Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 11, 2026  ·  Applies to the ChatFolder Extension and website

📌 Short version: We never read, store, or sell your ChatGPT conversations. We only store the folder structure and chat titles you organize — and only to sync your data across your own devices.

Thank you for using ChatFolder. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the ChatFolder Chrome Extension and the website located at https://chatfolder.chat.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Data

  • Email address — to identify your account and enable cross-device sync.
  • Display name — pulled from your Google account to personalize your experience.

1.2 Extension Data

  • Folder names and structure
  • Chat titles and URLs for organization
  • Bookmarks and private notes (synced across devices for logged-in users)
  • Prompt library entries

1.3 What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not read or store the content of your ChatGPT conversations.
  • We do not collect your browsing history.
  • We do not track activity on any website other than chatgpt.com.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate your account and sync data across devices.
  • Process and manage your premium subscription via Lemon Squeezy.
  • Send transactional emails and respond to support requests.

2a. Third-Party Data Processors

We use the following third-party services that may receive your personal data as part of providing the Service:

  • Lemon Squeezy (Payment Processor & Merchant of Record) — When you purchase a premium plan, your payment is handled by Lemon Squeezy. They receive your email address and billing information to complete the transaction and manage your subscription. Their data practices are governed by the Lemon Squeezy Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase (Database & Authentication) — Your account data and organised chat data are stored in Supabase's infrastructure. Supabase acts as a data processor on our behalf. Their data practices are governed by the Supabase Privacy Policy.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored locally in your browser and encrypted in our Supabase database for syncing. We use Row-Level Security (RLS) to ensure that only you can access your data.

4. Your Rights

You can delete your account at any time from the extension settings. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers. You can also request a copy of your data by contacting us.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this policy, please reach out to us at support@chatfolder.chat
