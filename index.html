Questions, answered

Everything you need to know before installing

Is ChatFolder really free? What's the catch?
No catch. The free plan gives you 3 folders, 5 bookmarks, and 3 saved prompts — enough to organize your most important workflows forever. Upgrade only when you need unlimited folders, the full prompt library, or cross-device sync. No credit card required to start.
Will it slow down ChatGPT or break with future updates?
ChatFolder is a lightweight Chrome extension built specifically for ChatGPT. It runs only on chatgpt.com, adds zero analytics or trackers to the page, and uses native browser storage so it doesn't touch your conversations. We update within 24-48 hours of any major ChatGPT redesign — you'll never be left with a broken tool.
Does ChatFolder read my chat messages?
No. ChatFolder only reads chat titles and the folder structure you create. The actual conversation content never leaves your browser, never reaches our servers, and is never logged. You can review the full privacy policy for details.
What happens to my folders if I cancel?
Your data is yours. If you cancel a paid plan, you keep full read access to all your folders, bookmarks, and prompts. You can also export everything as JSON at any time — no lock-in.
Can I get a refund?
Yes. 14-day money-back guarantee — no questions asked. Email support@chatfolder.chat within 14 days of purchase and you'll get a full refund processed by Lemon Squeezy.
Does it work on Safari, Firefox, or mobile?
Right now ChatFolder is built for Chrome and Chromium-based browsers (Edge, Brave, Arc, Opera). A Firefox version is on the roadmap. Mobile browsers don't support extensions — but your folders, bookmarks, and prompts sync across every Chrome desktop you use.
Lifetime — what does "forever" actually cover?
One payment of £79.99 unlocks every premium feature, plus every future update we ship, with no recurring charge ever. If we get acquired or shut down, you keep the version you have plus a full data export. That's the deal — written into our terms.
I'm not a "LinkedIn creator" — is this still for me?
Absolutely. ChatFolder works for anyone drowning in ChatGPT chats — students, researchers, devs, marketers, consultants, founders. The LinkedIn Creator Pack is one of 12 prompt categories. The folders, search, and sync work the same regardless of what you use ChatGPT for.
